# Italy Trip 2026 Planning Document

## Itinerary Overview

### Rome (6 days)
- Explore iconic landmarks and take family-friendly tours.
- Day trips to nearby historical sites.

### Naples (3 days)
- Discover local culture and enjoy the culinary delights.
- Possible day trips to islands or nearby attractions.

### Pompeii (1 day)
- Guided exploration of the archaeological site.
- Kid-friendly options and learning experiences.

### Amalfi Coast (5 days)
- Visit beautiful towns like Positano, Ravello, and Amalfi.
- Relaxing beach days and cultural exploration.

## Rome Highlights
- **Activities**: Explore the Colosseum, Vatican Museums, and the Roman Forum. Kids can enjoy hands-on activities at the Explora Children's Museum.
- **Restaurants**: Try traditional Roman dishes at Trattoria Vecchia Roma. Pizza and gelato break at Ciampini.

## Naples
- **Safety Tips**: Stick to well-known areas and be mindful of pickpockets. Avoid venturing into less crowded neighborhoods after dark.
- **Pizza Spots**: Visit L'Antica Pizzeria da Michele for authentic Neapolitan pizza.
- **Day Trips**: Consider a visit to Mount Vesuvius or take a ferry to the Isle of Capri.

## Pompeii
- **Visit Length**: Plan for 3-4 hours for a comprehensive visit. Even younger children will find the site engaging with a guide.
- **Best Time**: Mornings, to avoid the heat and crowds.

## Amalfi Coast
- **Towns to Visit**: Positano, Ravello, Amalfi. Each offers unique experiences and stunning vistas.
- **Transport Options**: Rent a car or use the SITA buses for convenience.
- **Beach Recommendations**: Visit Spiaggia Grande in Positano and Arienzo Beach Club for a day of sun and relaxation.

## Practical Information
- **Flights**: Direct flights from Jakarta to Rome are available. Check multiple airlines for the best rates in June.
- **Visa**: UK passport holders will require an ETIAS authorization from the end of 2026.
- **Travel Insurance**: Ensure comprehensive coverage for the family for peace of mind.
- **SIM Cards**: Check local providers for tourist-friendly plans offering data and calls.
- **Budget Estimates**: For a 3-week trip, budget approximately €14,000 - €15,000, considering accommodations, meals, transportation, and activities.

## Booking Timeline
- **Now**: Book flights, and major accommodations to secure the best deals.
- **3-6 Months Out**: Secure tickets to major attractions and organize any day trips or tours.
- **Closer to Date**: Finalize local transport and check for any updates on travel advisories.
