## 3. Normative Formatting Rules

These rules apply to every Candid Labs document to ensure consistency
across SOPs, databases, blueprints, user guides, and operational
manuals.

### 3.1 Layout

-   Page size: A4

-   Margins: Standard Google Docs margins

-   Orientation: Portrait

-   Header: Candid Labs logo (left-aligned)

-   Footer (required):

    -   "Candid Labs --- Internal Document"

    -   Version number

    -   Page number (Page X of Y)

### 3.2 Typography

-   **Font:** Google Sans (preferred) or Arial

-   **Body Size:** 11 pt

-   **Heading 1:** 20 pt, bold

-   **Heading 2:** 16 pt, bold

-   **Heading 3:** 13 pt, bold

-   **Line Spacing:** 1.15

-   **Paragraph Spacing:** 6 px before / 6 px after

### 3.3 Tables

-   Header row shaded light grey

-   Text centred for numeric data

-   Text left-aligned for general text

-   No merged cells except where unavoidable

-   Keep first row bold

-   Avoid unnecessary colours

## 9. Standard Naming Convention

All Candid Labs documents must follow this filename format:

CR Doc \[Number\] --- \[Title\] --- vX.X

Example:\

For templates or internal tools:\
CLDoc --- Template --- v1.0

## 10. Document Colour Palette

Use only approved palette:

-   **Candid Teal:** #2EBCCF

-   **Deep Navy:** #0F1C2C

-   **Slate Grey:** #738290

-   **Light Background Grey:** #EFEFEF

Avoid decorative colours. Stick to primary palette for diagrams and
highlights.

## 11. Diagram Standards

-   Use Lucidchart, Whimsical, or Google Drawings

-   Icons should be simple, flat, and minimal

-   Use teal for key objects, grey for supporting layers

-   Avoid gradients and shadows

## 12. Document Rules for NotebookLM Consumption

To ensure smooth processing:

-   Use clean, simple headings

-   Avoid overly nested tables

-   Ensure all diagrams have text labels

-   Provide a short summary at the top of complex documents

-   No emojis in final archived docs

-   Avoid large blocks of coloured backgrounds
