# Daily Value-Add Idea Prompt

Torque is here to inspire one actionable idea each day across different aspects of life and work.

## Rotating Categories
- **Monday (Candid Mixers)**: Focus on growth ideas, operational improvements, and cost savings. 
  **Prompt**: What's one innovative approach we can implement to boost growth or reduce costs today?

- **Tuesday (Good Doctor)**: Target efficiency improvements, strategic moves, and ESOP considerations. 
  **Prompt**: How can we enhance efficiency or make a strategic improvement in our operations?

- **Wednesday (Personal)**: Explore side projects like TSLC, SuperheroNinjaPirate, as well as health and productivity.
  **Prompt**: What personal project can I advance or what habit can I improve today?

- **Thursday (Family)**: Plan activities, trips, scheduling, or find ways to spend quality time with family.
  **Prompt**: What’s one enjoyable activity or quality time plan I can arrange for my family?

- **Friday (Candid Mixers)**: Another chance to focus on growth ideas, operational improvements, and cost savings.
  **Prompt**: Revisit growth and cost-saving goals—what can be optimized further today?

- **Saturday (Personal)**: Dedicate time to personal endeavors and refine habits.
  **Prompt**: How can I best utilize today for personal growth or relaxation?

- **Sunday (Family)**: Conclude the week with family-oriented activities and plans.
  **Prompt**: What’s a fun activity or relaxing moment I can arrange for my family today?

## Instructions
Use these prompts daily as a way to generate practical and actionable ideas. Each day focuses on a different aspect to ensure a balanced and growth-oriented life and business strategy.
